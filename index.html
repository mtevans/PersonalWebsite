A Little Bit About Me

I'm a JavaScript and Rails developer with experience in Flux, React.js, jQuery and Ruby. When I'm not coding my hobbies include, but are not limited to, losing lifelong friends through the medium of Settlers of Catan, filling out my clothes in pursuit of San Francisco's top breakfast sandwich, and doing whatever I can to get outside and change this fair fragile British complexion of mine.

In a previous life, because saying a couple months ago doesn't sound quite as chic, I was a professional rower for the Canadian National Team. However, after an intense and liberating spiritual journey i realized that were quicker ways to get across bodies of water than rowing (who'd have thought!!) so I packed up my small pile clothes and enormous pile of spandex and left Canada to persue my love for computer programming
